Alleppey Travel Itinerary

Discover the Serenity of Kerala's Backwaters

Your Ultimate Alleppey Experience

Alleppey, known as the 'Venice of the East,' is famous for its picturesque backwaters, tranquil beaches, and unique houseboat experiences. Explore the lush landscapes and local culture through this carefully curated itinerary.

Day 1: Exploring Alleppey

  • Morning: Alleppey Beach - Enjoy a walk along the serene Alleppey Beach and visit the historic Alappuzha Lighthouse.
  • Afternoon: Backwater Cruise - Embark on a houseboat or shikara ride through the backwaters.
  • Evening: Marari Beach - Relax by the sea at Marari Beach, famous for its coconut palms and golden sands.

Day 2: Cultural and Natural Attractions

  • Morning: Krishnapuram Palace - Explore this 16th-century palace and its archaeological museum.
  • Afternoon: Pathiramanal Island - Visit this small island known for its bird species and rich biodiversity.
  • Evening: Kuttanad Region - Witness the unique farming practices in the 'Rice Bowl of Kerala.'

Additional Activities

  • Coir Industry Visit - Discover the traditional coir weaving industry in Alleppey.
  • Toddy Shop Experience - Taste the local palm wine at a toddy shop.

Top Food Spots in Alleppey

  • Amma's Restaurant - Famous for its traditional Kerala Sadya and local dishes.
  • The Lighthouse Restaurant - Offering seafood delicacies with a view of the beach.
  • Thaff Restaurant - Known for its aromatic biryanis and Kerala-style fish curry.

Adventure Activities in Alleppey

  • Kayaking in the Backwaters - Paddle through the calm backwaters for an adventurous experience.
  • Cycling Tour in Kuttanad - Explore the scenic paddy fields and traditional village life.
  • Houseboat Adventure - Enjoy a unique night stay in a traditional houseboat on the backwaters.

Enjoy your trip to Alleppey, where culture, nature, and adventure await you!
